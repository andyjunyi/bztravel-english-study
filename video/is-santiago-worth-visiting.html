Present Simple for Describing Places ▾
現在簡單式廣泛用於描述地方的特徵、設施和常態。影片中用現在簡單式來介紹Santiago的各個街區及其特色。
The Mapocho River cuts right through Santiago, naturally dividing Barrio Lastarria from Barrio Bellavista.
使用現在簡單式 'cuts' 和 'dividing' 來描述河流的常態性功能。'naturally' 強調這是地理上的自然劃分。
[Geographical feature] + present simple verb + [preposition] + [location], [adverb] + verb-ing + [result].
This street is lined with great restaurants, wine bars, artisans and cafes, and local vendors.
被動語態 'is lined with' 描述街道兩旁的常態性景象。列出多種商家類型來豐富描述。
[Subject] is lined with [list of things].
用現在簡單式描述你最喜歡的街區。
'My neighborhood sits near the river and offers a mix of cafes, bookstores, and small parks. The streets are lined with old trees that provide shade in summer.'
Despite + Noun Phrase for Concession ▾
'Despite' 用於引導讓步狀語，表示儘管有某個條件，主要情況仍然成立。後面接名詞短語或動名詞。
Despite reading several online warnings, we felt totally safe walking around tonight.
'Despite reading...warnings' 是一個讓步短語，表示儘管有安全警告，他們仍然感到安全。'reading...warnings' 是動名詞短語。
Despite + [verb-ing / noun phrase], [subject] + [contrasting statement].
用 'Despite' 完成句子：'______ the rain, we still enjoyed our walk through the city.'
'Despite the rain, we still enjoyed our walk through the city.'
