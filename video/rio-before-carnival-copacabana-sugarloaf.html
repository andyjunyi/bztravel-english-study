點選文法點展開說明
Present Simple for Scheduled Events ▾
使用現在簡單式描述已安排好的未來事件，特別是依據行程表或日程的活動。影片中用 'Carnival will draw 6 million' 預測未來事件，但也用 'draws' 這類現在簡單式來描述規律性發生的事件。
Carnival will draw 6 million, the largest in the world.
使用未來式 'will draw' 來預測即將發生的事件（嘉年華的人數），同時用 'the largest' 進行比較。
[Event] will [verb] [number], the [superlative] in [the world/the country].
We're staying in Copacabana and we have a hotel that's right on the very front of the beach.
使用現在進行式 'are staying' 來描述當前正在進行的行動。'have' 使用現在簡單式，表示擁有的狀態。
Subject + be + verb-ing (current action) + and + subject + have + [description]
用未來式描述一個即將舉行的大型活動。
'The New Year's fireworks display will draw over 2 million people to the beach, one of the largest celebrations in the world.'
Contrast with 'but' and 'even with' ▾
使用 'but' 和 'even with' 來引入對比，強調儘管有不利條件，某事仍然成立。
The weather had other plans. Rain, gray skies, the kind of morning that sends beach crowds away. Surprising still with Carnival only days around the corner.
先描述不利的天氣條件，然後用 'Surprising still' 來強調儘管如此，這個情況仍然令人驚訝。'with Carnival only days around the corner' 進一步增加了對比。
[Adverse condition]. Surprising [adverb] with [context]...
用 'even with' 完成句子：'______ the rain, the beach was still beautiful.'
'Even with the rain, the beach was still beautiful.'
