條件句 — If you don't [A], your [N] will. ▾
這是一種口語化的條件句結構，省略了後半句的重複動詞。常用於表達因果或警告性的建議。
There's a saying, if you don't fly business, your kids will.
→ 'if you don't...your kids will' = 如果你不...你的孩子會 → 省略了後半句的 'fly business'
If you don't + V, your + N + will → "如果你不...你的...會"
We figured might as well enjoy it ourselves.
→ 'might as well' = 不妨；還不如 → 表示在某種情況下最好的選擇
might as well + V → "不妨...；還是...好了"
If you don't take the chance, ______ (別人會).
someone else will
動名詞做主詞 ▾
動名詞（V-ing）可以作為句子的主詞，表示一個抽象的概念或活動。這種結構比用 'To V' 做主詞更口語化。
Figuring out where to stay kind of had us scratching our heads.
→ 'Figuring out where to stay' = 動名詞片語當主詞 → 後面接 'had us scratching our heads' 表示導致的結果
V-ing + O + V + O → "做某事使得某人..."
______ (在陌生的城市找路) can be challenging.
Finding your way around a strange city / Navigating a strange city
