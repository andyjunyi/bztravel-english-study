1. The High Altitude Journey Begins ▾
Tresa and Zarko fly from Lima to Juliaca, stepping off the plane at 12,500 feet. They immediately notice the surprisingly thin air and low oxygen. Their journey continues toward the Bolivian border, stopping for lunch in Puno, which is known as Peru's folklore capital and the base for exploring Lake Titicaca's famous floating islands.
Tresa和Zarko從Lima飛往Juliaca，在12,500英尺的高度下了飛機。他們立刻注意到令人驚訝的稀薄空氣和低氧量。他們的旅程繼續前往玻利維亞邊境，在Puno停下來吃午餐，Puno被稱為秘魯的民俗首府，也是探索的的喀喀湖著名漂浮島嶼的基地。
2. Flight Delays and Missed Opportunities ▾
What should have been a one-hour flight to Juliaca took seven hours. Thick fog forced the plane back to Lima to refuel and wait. As a result, they missed visiting the floating islands entirely, and could only manage a late lunch in Puno. Despite the setbacks, they describe Puno as a very cute town they would gladly explore with more time.
原本應該是一小時到Juliaca的航班花了七個小時。濃霧迫使飛機返回Lima加油和等待。結果，他們完全錯過了參觀漂浮島嶼，只能在Puno吃個遲來的午餐。儘管遭遇了這些挫折，他們形容Puno是一個非常可愛的小鎮，如果有更多時間，他們很樂意探索。
3. Lake Titicaca's Fresh Trout ▾
In Puno, the couple enjoys a lunch of fresh trout from Lake Titicaca, the world's highest navigable lake. The trout is described as rich, juicy, and orange in color, surprisingly looking and tasting much more like fresh salmon. This meal becomes a highlight despite the travel difficulties.
在Puno，這對夫婦享用了來自的的喀喀湖的新鮮鱒魚午餐，這是世界上海拔最高的可航行湖泊。鱒魚被形容為豐富、多汁、呈橘色，令人驚訝地看起來和吃起來都更像新鮮的鮭魚。儘管旅途困難，這頓飯成了一個亮點。
4. Dealing with Altitude Sickness ▾
To fight altitude sickness, the couple drinks coca tea and buys coca candy, which mildly improves oxygen uptake. After crossing the border into Bolivia, the thin air becomes even more noticeable. Walking across the border feels like climbing a mountain, with profound weakness, headaches, and dizziness affecting them.
為了對抗高山症，這對夫婦喝了古柯茶，並購買了古柯糖，這能輕微改善氧氣吸收。穿過邊境進入玻利維亞後，稀薄的空氣變得更加明顯。步行穿越邊境感覺就像爬山一樣，伴隨著嚴重的無力感、頭痛和頭暈。
5. Crossing into Bolivia ▾
The border crossing from Peru to Bolivia is an experience in itself. They walk across with the massive Lake Titicaca to their left, passing farmers working small quinoa and potato plots. The thin air makes even walking feel like a strenuous activity. Once in Bolivia, they head to Copacabana, where the high-altitude adventure continues.
從秘魯到玻利維亞的邊境過境本身就是一種體驗。他們步行穿過，左側是的的喀喀湖，經過在小型藜麥和馬鈴薯田裡工作的農民。稀薄的空氣使得即使是走路也感覺像劇烈運動。進入玻利維亞後，他們前往Copacabana，高海拔探險仍在繼續。

Past Simple for Sequential Narrative ▾
使用簡單過去式來按時間順序敘述事件。這種敘事結構清楚地展示了事件的先後順序，使讀者能夠跟隨旅行者的經歷。
We just flew from Lima to Juliaca. Stepping off the plane at 12,500 ft, we immediately noticed the surprisingly thin air.
使用 'flew' 和 'noticed' 兩個簡單過去式動詞來敘述連續事件。'Stepping off' 是現在分詞短語，表示伴隨動作。
Subject + simple past + [action]. [Verb-ing] + [detail], subject + simple past + [observation].
We planned to visit, but our 1-hour flight to Juliaca took 7 hours. Thick fog forced our plane back to Lima to refuel and wait. Since we missed the islands completely, we're just having a late lunch in Puno.
混合使用過去式（planned, took, forced, missed）和現在進行式（'re having）來表達從過去到現在的時間過渡。'Since' 表示原因。
Subject + planned to [verb], but [unexpected event] happened. [Adverse condition] + forced + [result]. Since [consequence], [current situation].
用簡單過去式按時間順序描述一個旅行經歷中的三次事件。
'We arrived at the airport at 6 AM. The flight was delayed by three hours. We finally landed at noon, exhausted but excited.'
Comparative Adjectives for Contrast ▾
比較級形容詞用於對比不同的事物或狀態。影片中使用比較級來強調高海拔環境與正常環境的差異。
This rich, juicy, orange trout, surprisingly looks and tastes much more like fresh salmon.
'Much more like' 是比較級結構，將湖鱒魚的味道和口感與鮭魚進行比較。'Surprisingly' 修飾整個句子，表達意外之情。
[Subject] looks and tastes [comparative] like [something else].
用 'much more like' 比較兩個相似的事物。
'This local cheese tastes much more like the one I had in France than the processed kind from the supermarket.'
