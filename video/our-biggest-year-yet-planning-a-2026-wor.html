在探索了超過100個國家之後，BZ Travel從未計劃過像今年這樣的一年。在這部影片中，Tresa和Zarko分享了他們整個2026年的旅行日曆，並徵求觀眾的意見。他們的行程排得滿滿的——從一月的祕魯和玻利維亞開始，接著是智利、阿根廷、巴西、阿曼或印度、中國和南非。他們已經預訂了幾個由G Adventures和Intrepid Travel等高端旅行社安排的小團行程，並希望觀眾提供建議來填補空缺。
2. Introduction ▾
We've explored over 100 countries, but we've never planned a year quite like this. Welcome back to BZ Travel. I'm Tresa, and I'm Zarko. This year, we are actually planning the entire year of our travels and we want to get some of your comments — we're kind of stuck. Our confirmed plans start from Bulgaria in January, heading to Peru and Bolivia. We're doing an extensive 15-day trip with G Adventures — the premium National Geographic Journeys small group tour. One of the highlights we definitely wanted to do this time is of course see Machu Picchu. From there, we'll go to some other really exciting places like the Salt Flats, Potosí for the silver mines, Lake Titicaca, and La Paz. All of this is high altitude travel, so it will be a little chilly and we might struggle a bit.
今年從為期15天的G Adventures國家地理祕魯和玻利維亞之旅開始。亮點包括馬丘比丘、烏尤尼鹽沼、波托西銀礦、的的喀喀湖和拉巴斯——全都在高海拔地區。接下來是智利和阿根廷的Intrepid Travel行程：瓦爾帕萊索、門多薩品酒、布宜諾斯艾利斯探戈，最後從阿根廷和巴西兩側欣賞伊瓜蘇瀑布。行程在里約熱內盧結束，額外三天自由探索科帕卡巴納和伊帕內瑪。
3. South America: Chile & Argentina ▾
After Bolivia, we'll fly down to Santiago. From there, we're meeting up with Intrepid Travel — another small group, their premium level. We'll be exploring Valparaíso for two days, then fly to Mendoza, Argentina, where Zarko is looking forward to the great wine and steaks. Mendoza is the home of the Malbec grape, and we'll be exploring three wineries there. Then we'll fly to Buenos Aires and stay at a nice lodge there. From there, we'll drive up to Rio — Zarko's been before and he's looking forward to it. We'll spend three days with the group: Christ the Redeemer, Sugarloaf Mountain, a complete city tour. After the tour, we're going to spend three more days on our own exploring Ipanema and Copacabana. They'll be setting up for Carnival the following weekend when we leave, so we plan to leave early. If you're familiar with Rio and have some great suggestions — particularly around Búzios or the islands — please leave some comments and give us some ideas.
在二月中旬回家剪輯影片後，團隊計劃在三月中旬再次出發。他們正在認真考慮阿曼——被形容為一顆未被發現的寶石，每週五早上在尼茲瓦鎮有迷人的駱駝拍賣。他們在討論要租車還是聘請私人司機。印度和菲律賓也在考慮之中。這對夫妻請觀眾提供關於阿曼路況和必看景點的建議。
4. Spring: Oman & China ▾
Mid-March we'll be itching to get back out there. We're planning on going to Oman — it sounds very interesting, kind of like an undiscovered gem. We're also considering India or the Philippines, but Oman is our strong lean. One question we have: should we rent a car and drive ourselves, or get a private driver? Thinking of using Muscat as a base. After Oman, about 10 days, then we'll be home for a couple of weeks. Then we want to go to China — Zarko found a great private 30-day immersive tour. You see everything: Xi'an and the terracotta warriors, the pandas, Tibet — pretty much all the major things. The tour ends in Hong Kong. We used to have an agent in Shenzhen and we've been there many times — we'd love to see him again and revisit the painting village there.
最令人興奮的計劃之一是為期30天的中國和西藏私人全包旅行。行程涵蓋一切：從西安的兵馬俑到成都的熊貓、萬里長城和西藏。由於他們之前因商務訪問中國，這將是他們第一次以遊客身份體驗中國。他們還想重訪深圳附近的一個畫村，他們曾在那裡採購藝術品並愛上了當地的畫廊。
5. Summer: Serbia & South Africa Safari ▾
After June, we'll come home for about a month, then mid-July we want to go back to South Africa for another safari. I absolutely fell in love with the animals, the beautiful lodges, and the incredible service. Nobody beats the private reserves in South Africa. The difference between a private reserve and a national park: in the park you're very restricted — you're with 20 other vehicles on the road, you cannot get off the road. In a private reserve, you have a tracker and go with the Land Cruiser straight through the bush. We're also thinking of a short Serbia bike trip — riding the bike paths in Niš and Belgrade. It's right next door and we haven't been there. That's kind of a shame.
六月，朋友們將訪問保加利亞，團隊計劃騎電動自行車去塞爾維亞。七月，他們將重返南非進行另一次野生動物獵遊——團隊在上次旅行中愛上了私人野生動物保護區。整部影片中，Tresa和Zarko反覆請求觀眾提供建議：巴西住哪裡、是否在阿曼開車，以及還有哪些隱藏寶石值得造訪。這部影片既是旅行計劃，也是與觀眾的對話。
6. Late Year: South Pacific & Open Questions ▾
Now the next one is where we'd really like some suggestions. I've been wanting to go to the South Pacific for a long time. On our world cruise we went to Tahiti and Moorea, but we were only there for 4 hours. It was the most beautiful thing I've ever seen. I want to go back. Two options: we could go on the Aranui 5, a freighter that goes into small bays on remote islands — meeting villagers, nothing touristy. Or we could take the Oceania luxury cruise ship and be in the lap of luxury. We're leaning toward the Aranui 5 for the authentic experience, but there's very little traveler content about it online, so it feels like a risk. If you've been on that ship or know of any information, please tell us. We're planning around the end of September. That leaves October, November, and December wide open. Where should we go? We have the whole world in front of us.

📐 文法重點 2 點
點選文法點展開說明
現在完成式 + 表示經驗的 ever/never ▾
現在完成式（have + p.p.）用來表達從過去到現在的經歷。'Have you ever...' 詢問是否有過某經驗；'have never been' 表示從未有過某經驗。
We've explored over 100 countries, but we've never planned a year quite like this.
→ 'We've explored' = 到目前為止已經探索過 → 'have never planned' = 從未計劃過 → 'I've never been there' = 從未去過
S + have/has + p.p. + but + have/has + never + p.p. → 「已經...但從未...」
I ______ (去過) many countries, but I ______ (從未去過) South America.
have visited / have never been to
No + 比較級 + than... → 最高級意味 ▾
'Nobody beats...' 的意思是「沒有人比...更好」，等於最高級的表達方式，但更有強調效果。
Nobody beats the private reserves in South Africa.
→ Nobody beats = Nobody is better than → 等於 'Private reserves in South Africa are the best'
Nobody beats + [名詞] + in [類別] → 「在...方面沒有人比...更好」
______ (沒有人比得上) the service at this hotel in all of Southeast Asia.
Nobody beats
