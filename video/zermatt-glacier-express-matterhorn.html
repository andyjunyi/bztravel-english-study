1. Zermatt, Glacier Express & Matterhorn ▾

三個瑞士傳奇等著我們——策馬特、雄偉的馬特洪峰和冰川快車。策馬特已實施無車政策數十年。遊客必須將車停在5公里外，搭乘接駁火車進城。城內只允許電動計程車和小型電動巴士行駛，即使在海拔高處也保持街道安靜和空氣清新。策馬特以其阿爾卑斯通道聞名——纜車通往各個方向，直達歐洲最高的纜車站。
2. Arriving in Zermatt ▾
Welcome back. Three Swiss legends await: Zermatt, the Mighty Matterhorn, and the Glacier Express ride. Today's journey starts in Zermatt. Zermatt has been car-free for decades. Visitors must park in Täsch, 5 km away, and ride a shuttle train into town. Inside Zermatt, only electric taxis and small electric buses are allowed — keeping the streets quiet and the air crisp even at altitude. Zermatt is renowned for its alpine access. Cable cars go in all directions — we're heading straight up to the highest cable car station in Europe. Zermatt sits at 1,620 m; Klein Matterhorn at 3,883 m. Swiss cable cars don't mess around — these gondolas hold 100 people and run with terrifying precision, hauling 2,000 skiers an hour in winter. This place isn't just pretty, it's a machine. 54 lifts, 360 km of trails, three mountain zones, and a glacier playground up top. You can literally ski from Switzerland to Italy, cross the border at 3,480 m, and be back for lunch. We're here in the off-season — quiet, cold, and perfect. No lines, no crowds. Just us and the Alps.
團隊前往海拔3,883公尺的小馬特洪峰。瑞士纜車可不是開玩笑的——這些纜車可容納100人，以驚人的精準度運行。要抵達山頂，他們需要轉乘三條纜車，一條比一條陡峭。放眼望去，盡是纜車和讓人胃部翻攪的滑雪道。這個系統冬季每小時運送2,000名滑雪客。從纜車內部望去，景色令人目瞪口呆——他們在原地轉圈，試圖把所有美景都收進眼底。
3. Klein Matterhorn: Europe's Highest Cable Car ▾
To reach the Klein Matterhorn, we switch through three lifts, each one steeper than the last. Everywhere you look: chair lifts, cable cars, and runs that make your stomach drop just watching. No wonder this place ranks among the top ski spots on the planet. You can ski this mountain every single day of the year — not a crusty patch of snow, but 21 km of groomed runs across a real glacier. In September, while the rest of Europe is lounging in t-shirts, Olympic teams are up here grinding it out. The final climb: the Matterhorn Glacier Express. This beast takes you up Europe's highest cable car stretch. Floating over the Theodul glacier, we spot the year-round ski zone — a training mecca for pro racers. Then the doors open at 3,883 m and the air is thin, dry, weird. You feel it hit your lungs. The panoramic view is remarkable. Switzerland on one side, Italy on the other, Mont Blanc — the tallest peak in the Alps — stands clearly visible to the west. A vast expanse of snow-covered peaks surrounds you. The most striking is the Matterhorn, seen from an uncommon angle almost level with its shoulder — offering a rare perspective of the mountain's dramatic north face, a nearly vertical wall of rock and ice, infamous for its difficulty and the number of climbers who have perished attempting to scale it.
在3,883公尺的高度，空氣稀薄乾燥，直接衝擊你的肺部。全景視野令人驚嘆——一邊是瑞士，另一邊是義大利，西邊可以清楚看到白朗峰。最引人注目的是馬特洪峰本身，從一個不常見的角度觀看，幾乎與它的山肩平行。這個位置提供了其戲劇性北壁的罕見視角——一面幾乎垂直的岩石和冰壁，因許多嘗試攀登它而喪生的登山者數量而惡名昭彰。
4. Zermatt Evening ▾
Zermatt pops back into view — tiny, cozy, full of chalet-style hotels. It's quiet now, but winter brings 15,000 skiers into this sleepy town. Evening walk and dinner ahead. It's the off-season — lots of boutique shops closed, high-end winter gear, thermal layers, down jackets, ski equipment, and luxury Swiss watches in the windows. This reflects the low season; during the winter months, these same streets are likely bustling well into the night. Ending the day right: Swiss steak, French wine, and a warm mountain restaurant. Nothing fancy, just perfect.
登上冰川快車，就像踏入軌道上的優雅世界。頭等艙、全景車窗，前排座位觀賞瑞士阿爾卑斯山的展開。這列火車是為景觀而建——巨大的玻璃窗 curved 延伸到車頂。與其說是交通工具，不如說是一個穿越山脈的玻璃泡泡。路線包括91條隧道和291座橋樑。上阿爾卑斯山口爬升到2,033公尺，是歐洲最高的鐵路路線之一。工程設計和景觀一樣令人屏息。
5. The Glacier Express ▾
Today we leave Zermatt behind and board the Glacier Express — 8 hours of pure alpine theater. This isn't just transit, it's elegance on rails. First class, panoramic windows, a front-row seat as the Swiss Alps unfold. Slow and spectacular, all the way to St. Moritz. This train doesn't just cross Switzerland, it conquers it. 91 tunnels, 291 bridges, and a route carved over half a century. The Oberalp Pass climbs to 2,033 m — one of Europe's highest rail lines. The engineering is as dramatic as the landscape. The Glacier Express experience is notably unhurried but never tedious. The slow, continuous movement creates a calming rhythm. Time seems to pass without notice. Rather than boredom, travelers often report a sense of relaxation and mindfulness. Then comes the moment: the Landwasser Viaduct. This thing is everywhere — posters, postcards, Instagram. A 136 m curved limestone bridge that disappears into a cliff like it's no big deal. You're riding on the image people picture when they think Swiss Alps. And it lives up to its hype. The train winds through loop tunnels buried deep in the rock, spiraling upward. Inside, time and direction collapse — you're corkscrew through mountain marrow. Then suddenly daylight. The valley is now beneath you. It's disorienting in the most beautiful way.
冰川快車從策馬特到聖莫里茨需要8小時。緩慢而壯觀。這種體驗從容不迫但絕不無聊。乘客可以欣賞到連綿不絕的雄偉山脈、阿爾卑斯山谷、迷人的村莊和歷史悠久的橋樑。持續的視覺刺激加上平穩的行駛產生了冥想般的效果。最後，團隊反思了淡季的寧靜——沒有排隊、沒有人潮，只有他們和阿爾卑斯山。

比較級疊加 — each + 比較級 + than the last (一個比一個更...) ▾
用 'each + 比較級 + than the last' 來描述一系列事物逐次遞增的程度，是一種優雅的英文修辭結構。
To reach the Klein Matterhorn, we switch through three lifts. Each one steeper than the last.
→ Each one steeper than the last = 第三條比第二條陡，第二條比第一條陡 → 強調逐漸增加的變化
Each + N + 比較級 + than the last → 「一個比一個更...」
We visited three museums in one day. ______ (一個比一個更令人印象深刻).
Each one more impressive than the last
S + V + 名詞片語 (同位語補充說明) ▾
用名詞片語放在句尾補充說明主詞或受詞的身份或性質。同位語（appositive）是一個名詞或名詞片語放在另一個名詞旁邊，用來補充說明它的身份。
The Matterhorn — that mountain that's on that Toblerone candy bar.
→ 'that mountain...' 是 The Matterhorn 的同位語 → 補充說明馬特洪峰的身份
[名詞] — [同位語補充說明]
Zermatt — ______ (那個禁止車輛通行的阿爾卑斯小鎮) — is a paradise for nature lovers.
that car-free alpine town
