Past Perfect Tense for Background Information ▾
過去完成式用於描述在另一個過去事件之前已經發生的情況。在本影片中，「We had read things, watched the news... We thought we had a reasonable picture」使用過去完成式來強調在抵達中國之前就已經形成的認知。
We had a theory about China. We had read things, watched the news, talked to people who had been there.
had read, had watched, had talked — 這些過去完成式動詞描述在他們實際訪問中國之前所做的事情。主句使用簡單過去式（had a theory），而從句使用過去完成式來表示更早發生的動作。
Subject + had + past participle + (object), before + subject + simple past...
He grew up in Bulgaria under communism. Not reading about it, living it.
使用簡單過去式（grew up）敘述過去的事實。然後用現在分詞（reading, living）進行對比，強調親身經歷與間接了解的差異。
Subject + simple past + ... Not + verb-ing + (about) + object, verb-ing + object
將以下句子改寫為過去完成式：'I read about the country before I visited it.'
'I had read about the country before I visited it.'
Comparative Structures for Contrast ▾
比較結構用於對比兩個人物、觀點或經歷。影片中頻繁使用比較來凸顯Tresa和Zarko之間不同的背景和視角。
Zarko's story is different. He grew up in Bulgaria under communism.
使用 'is different' 引入對比。然後詳細解釋Zarko的經歷如何與Tresa的經歷形成對比。這是一種常見的篇章組織方式。
A's story/narrative/experience is different. A + verb + (details of difference)...
I'm American... which means I grew up with a pretty specific picture of the world.
使用 'which means' 引入解釋或結果。這裡用來解釋Tresa的成長背景如何塑造了她的世界觀。
Subject + be + nationality/identity, which means + subject + grew up with + noun phrase
完成句子：'My experience was different from my friend's. I grew up in a small town, which means ________.'
'My experience was different from my friend's. I grew up in a small town, which means I had a simpler view of the world.'
