1. The Ultimate Safari Dream ▾
After three weeks and over 40 game drives, the travelers share their ultimate safari experience. They take viewers deep into the Klaserie Nature Reserve, where Simba Game Lodge is located on the edge of the Greater Kruger area. With only eight suites, there are no crowds here. The lodge is stylish and exclusive, with prime access to Africa's most iconic wildlife. It is hard to believe that this exists in the middle of nowhere.
經過三週和超過40場的遊獵體驗後，旅行者們分享他們的終極safari體驗。他們帶領觀眾深入克拉塞里自然保護區，辛巴野生動物旅館就坐落在大克魯格地區的邊緣。僅有八間套房，這裡沒有擁擠的人群。旅館時尚而獨特，可以近距離接觸非洲最具代表性的野生動物。很難相信這一切存在於荒野之中。
2. Camp George: Luxury in the Wild ▾
Camp George blends modern comfort with wild Africa. Step outside and you are in untamed nature; inside, tasteful decor and pure comfort await. From the private deck, there is a dry riverbed and an electric fence separating the camp from the wilderness. The travelers enjoy lunch facing a water hole, South African wine in hand, watching baboons outside the fence. It is a perfect prelude to their afternoon siesta before the next game drive.
喬治營地融合了現代舒適與狂野非洲。踏出戶外，你就置身於未馴服的大自然中；室內則是品味裝潢和純粹的舒適。從私人露台可以看到乾涸的河床和將營地與荒野隔開的電網。旅行者們面對水塘享用午餐，手拿南非葡萄酒，看著圍欄外的狒狒。這是下午小憩前完美的序幕，為下一場遊獵做準備。
3. Extraordinary First Encounters ▾
On the very first game drive, the travelers come across something incredibly rare — a female leopard with a freshly killed impala, bigger than she is. She was right beside the vehicle, dragging the impala toward a tree. The strength of these animals is unreal. Unfortunately, during the night, two hyenas stole her prize. Up the road, two rhinos graze — their horns cut to deter poachers, but even hornless, they are massive, powerful, and incredibly majestic.
在第一次遊獵中，旅行者們遇到了極為罕見的一幕——一頭雌性花豹帶著一頭剛殺死的黑斑羚，這獵物比牠自己還大。牠就在車輛旁邊，拖著黑斑羚朝一棵樹走去。這些動物的力量超乎現實。不幸的是，夜間兩隻鬣狗偷走了牠的戰利品。沿路而上，兩頭犀牛在吃草——牠們的角被割掉以阻止盜獵者，但即使沒有角，牠們仍然龐大、強壯且極其威嚴。
4. A Day in the Life of a Safari ▾
A safari day mirrors a lion's life: hunt, sleep, and eat. The mornings start early with game drives, followed by gourmet breakfasts. The chef at Camp George makes everything from scratch — banana muffins, cappuccino, eggs, bacon, all gourmet. The travelers learn fascinating facts: hippos secrete a pink antiseptic oil to protect their skin, wildebeest and zebras eat different parts of the grass, and elephant dung can be used to start a fire.
safari的一天反映了獅子的生活：狩獵、睡覺和吃東西。清晨早早開始遊獵，接著是美食早餐。喬治營地的廚師從頭開始製作一切——香蕉鬆餅、卡布奇諾、雞蛋、培根，全都是美食級的。旅行者們學到了有趣的知識：河馬分泌一種粉紅色的防腐油來保護皮膚，角馬和斑馬吃草的不同部位，大象的糞便可以用來生火。
5. The Big Cats of Klaserie ▾
Klaserie Nature Reserve delivers on its promise of big cat encounters. A handsome lion with scratches on his nose from battles with rival males soaks up the morning sun. Leopards demonstrate their incredible strength, hauling prey twice their weight up into the branches. The wild becomes a theater of drama — every bend could reveal something incredible. The travelers feel incredibly lucky to witness these moments, and their love for Africa grows with every encounter.
克拉塞里自然保護區實現了大型貓科動物邂逅的承諾。一頭英俊的獅子鼻子上的抓痕訴說著與競爭雄獅的戰鬥，享受著早晨的陽光。花豹展示了令人難以置信的力量，將比自身重兩倍的獵物拖上樹枝。荒野成為了一齣戲劇——每一個轉彎都可能展現出不可思議的景象。旅行者們感到無比幸運能見證這些時刻，他們對非洲的熱愛隨著每一次邂逅而增長。

現在完成式 — 表達經驗與成就 ▾
現在完成式 (have/has + p.p.) 用於表達過去的經驗對現在的影響或結果。在旅遊影片中常用來總結經驗。
We spent three weeks and we went on 40 plus game drives.
→ 'spent' 和 'went' 都是過去簡單式，描述已完成的過去事件 → 可以用現在完成式改寫：We have spent three weeks...
S + have/has + p.p. + [時間/數量] → "已經做了某事..."
I've never been this close to a leopard before.
→ 'have never been' = 從未...過 → 現在完成式 + ever/never 表達人生經驗
I've never been this + 形容詞 + before → "我從未如此...過"
I ______ (從未見過) such a beautiful sunset before.
have never seen
讓步子句 — Even + N/adj + ... ▾
'Even' 可以修飾名詞或形容詞，表達「即使...也...」的讓步關係，用來加強反差。
Even hornless, they're massive, powerful, and incredibly majestic.
→ 'Even hornless' = 即使沒有角 → 省略了 'though they are' → Even (though they are) hornless
Even + 形容詞, S + V → "即使...，也..."
Even the flight becomes a safari.
→ 'Even + N' = 連...也 → 強調達到了出人意料的程度
Even + 名詞 + V → "連...都..."
______ (即使下雨), we continued our hike.
Even in the rain / Even though it was raining
