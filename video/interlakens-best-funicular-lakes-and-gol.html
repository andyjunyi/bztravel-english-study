2. Arriving in Interlaken ▾
Come along to Interlaken, one of Switzerland's famous destinations, and ride on the Golden Pass Express. We're spending 8 days riding every panoramic train across Switzerland, stopping in postcard-perfect places along the way. We've just rolled in to Interlaken from Lucerne. This city is literally sandwiched between two lakes with mountains wrapping around it — like nature went all in on the drama. Everywhere I look, paragliders are floating down from the cliffs. At first we thought it was some festival thing, but nope — just Tuesday in Interlaken. Dozens of tandem flights, Swiss precision everywhere. Interlaken: small, super walkable, and every hotel hands you a free bus pass. The weather we got was unbelievable — the waiter said we were the luckiest couple in the world to be there at that time.
出發前兩週，天氣預報顯示每天都在下雨。團隊打包了雨衣、雨傘和防水鞋，做好了最壞的打算。但當他們抵達時，天氣卻完美無缺。連服務生都說：「這太不可思議了。你們是世界上最幸運的情侶，能在這個時間來到這裡。」團隊反思說，人很容易被完美的天氣迷惑，然後視為理所當然。陽光讓因特拉肯著名的景色變得絕對令人驚嘆。
3. Paragliding & Funicular ▾
The paragliding was about $200 for 1 hour — the actual ride itself is 20 minutes, with a weight limit of around 220 lbs. Most of the people doing it are women. These days most guys are way over 250 pounds. You can't skip the funicular ride — it climbs straight up the mountain and rewards you with unreal views over Interlaken and both lakes. The funicular here is no joke: it climbs the mountain at an angle that feels more roller coaster than train. You shoot through a tunnel halfway up. Smooth, quiet, and precise. By the time you reach the top, your ears pop, your jaw drops. At the top of Harder Kulm, there's even a restaurant — a perfect spot to grab a beer or hot chocolate and zone out at that view. The platform juts straight off the cliff. Nothing but air beneath you. Mountains all around. Lakes glimmering below.
因特拉肯有幾條纜車可以將遊客載往全景觀景台。這座城市小巧且極適合步行，每家酒店都會給客人一張免費的巴士通行證。從高處俯瞰，兩座湖泊——圖恩湖和布里恩茨湖——的景色壯麗無比，碧綠的湖水與綠色的山谷和白雪覆蓋的山峰形成對比。齒軌纜車本身就是工程奇蹟，以瑞士式的精準攀爬陡峭的坡度。
4. The View from the Top ▾
From up here, the geography finally makes sense. Thun on one side, Brienz on the other, and Interlaken right in the middle. Snowy peaks, blue lakes, and that skinny river connecting them. It's like nature's perfect layout. Indescribable. Interlaken has it all — hiking trails, shimmering lakes, and in winter, world-class skiing just minutes away. Summer's lively, winter's pure postcard perfection. It's a kind of town that feels genuinely Swiss: peaceful, tidy, and surrounded by ridiculous beauty. Easily one of the best spots we've seen.
金色山口快車是瑞士風景最美的全景列車路線之一。從盧塞恩經因特拉肯到蒙特勒，旅程帶領乘客穿越八個瑞士邦。列車擁有現代化的全景車廂，從地板到天花板的窗戶提供無障礙的景觀視野。路線穿過伯爾尼高地的心臟地帶，沿途可以看到著名的艾格峰、僧侶峰和少女峰。
5. Golden Pass Express to Montreux ▾
Good morning. Today we're taking the Golden Pass Express — a panoramic train going to Montreux, the French part of Switzerland. Right now we're in the German part, where last night we had sausage and German beer. Tonight we'll have wine and hopefully a beautiful French dinner. Montreux is famous — Freddie Mercury used to live there. There's a monument. Our hotel will be right on the shore of Lake Geneva. The Golden Pass Express isn't just transport — it's a front-row seat to Switzerland changing moods in 3 hours. You go from alpine peaks to Riviera vibes. The ride starts along Lake Thun, deep teal water and tiny villages sliding past like scenes from a painting. Huge wooden homes with slanted roofs, wide balconies, and bright flowers under every window. The countryside feels timeless. We're in Spiez. At this point they're going to actually change the cogs on the train so we can start climbing steeper. They've engineered a process where they can literally just change out the wheels — and no one even knows it's happening. One turn and suddenly the mountain ends. Straight below, Lake Geneva spreads out like an endless shimmering sea. Palm trees. Yes, really. Now I see why they call it the Swiss Riviera. The cows and meadows are gone, replaced by vineyards and palm trees. Same country. Totally different world.
因特拉肯是為期八天、搭乘每條瑞士全景火車的旅程的一部分。團隊沿途在明信片般完美的地方停留，體驗瑞士傳奇的鐵路系統。從金色山口快車到攀爬周圍山峰的纜車，瑞士的交通和目的地本身一樣是體驗的一部分。瑞士人不只是開車——他們滑行，每一段旅程都變成一場風景冒險。

It looks [Adj], but + S + [V] + is another story (看起來...，但...是另一回事) ▾
用 'is another story' 表達某事看起來很好但實際做起來又是另一回事，帶有幽默的轉折。
It looks epic, but still leaping off a perfectly solid mountain — I'd probably freeze halfway to the edge.
→ 'looks epic' = 看起來很壯觀 → 用 'but' 轉折引出實際情況：臨陣退縮
It looks + [Adj], but + [實際情況] + is another story
Paragliding looks amazing, ______ (但真的要跳下去是另一回事).
but actually jumping is another story
You're the most + Adj + N + to + V (你是最...的...) ▾
最高級 + 不定詞片語，用來強調某人在某方面是最特別的。
You're the most luckiest couple in the world to be here at this time.
→ 'the luckiest couple in the world' = 世界上最好的情侶 → 'to be here' = 不定詞修飾前面，說明「在哪方面最幸運」
You're the + 最高級 + N + to V
You're the ______ (最勇敢的人) to climb that mountain in this weather.
bravest person
