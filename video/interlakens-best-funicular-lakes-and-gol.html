1. Interlaken: Sandwiched Between Two Lakes ▾
The team rolls into Interlaken from Lucerne. This city is literally sandwiched between two lakes with mountains wrapping around it — nature went all in on the drama. Interlaken is one of Switzerland's most famous destinations. Everywhere you look, paragliders are floating down from the cliffs. At first, the team thought it was some festival, but no — just Tuesday in Interlaken. Dozens of tandem flights, safety briefings, Swiss precision everywhere. It looks epic, though leaping off a perfectly solid mountain is another story.
團隊從盧塞恩抵達因特拉肯。這座城市 literally 夾在兩座湖泊之間，群山環繞——大自然把戲劇效果發揮到了極致。因特拉肯是瑞士最著名的目的地之一。無論你往哪裡看，都有滑翔傘從懸崖上飄落下來。起初團隊以為是什麼節日活動，但其實只是因特拉肯的星期二。幾十組雙人飛行、安全簡報，瑞士精準隨處可見。雖然從一座完好的山上跳下去是另一回事，但看起來確實很壯觀。
2. A Lucky Weather Miracle ▾
Two weeks before the trip, the weather forecast showed rain every single day. The team packed raincoats, umbrellas, and waterproof shoes, preparing for the worst. But when they arrived, the weather was perfect. Even the waiter said, 'This is unbelievable. You're the luckiest couple in the world to be here at this time.' The team reflects on how easy it is to get seduced by perfect weather and take it for granted. The sunshine made Interlaken's famous views absolutely stunning.
出發前兩週，天氣預報顯示每天都在下雨。團隊打包了雨衣、雨傘和防水鞋，做好了最壞的打算。但當他們抵達時，天氣卻完美無缺。連服務生都說：「這太不可思議了。你們是世界上最幸運的情侶，能在這個時間來到這裡。」團隊反思說，人很容易被完美的天氣迷惑，然後視為理所當然。陽光讓因特拉肯著名的景色變得絕對令人驚嘆。
3. The Funicular and Panoramic Views ▾
Interlaken offers several funiculars that carry visitors up to panoramic viewpoints. The city is small and super walkable, and every hotel hands guests a free bus pass. From the heights, the views of the two lakes — Lake Thun and Lake Brienz — are spectacular, with the turquoise water contrasting against the green valley and snow-capped peaks. The cogwheel funiculars themselves are engineering marvels, climbing steep gradients with Swiss precision.
因特拉肯有幾條纜車可以將遊客載往全景觀景台。這座城市小巧且極適合步行，每家酒店都會給客人一張免費的巴士通行證。從高處俯瞰，兩座湖泊——圖恩湖和布里恩茨湖——的景色壯麗無比，碧綠的湖水與綠色的山谷和白雪覆蓋的山峰形成對比。齒軌纜車本身就是工程奇蹟，以瑞士式的精準攀爬陡峭的坡度。
4. The Golden Pass Express: A Journey in Itself ▾
The Golden Pass Express is one of Switzerland's most scenic panoramic train routes. Running from Lucerne to Montreux via Interlaken, the journey takes passengers through eight Swiss cantons. The train features modern panoramic cars with floor-to-ceiling windows that offer unobstructed views of the landscape. The route passes through the heart of the Bernese Oberland, with its famous peaks — Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau — visible along the way.
金色山口快車是瑞士風景最美的全景列車路線之一。從盧塞恩經因特拉肯到蒙特勒，旅程帶領乘客穿越八個瑞士邦。列車擁有現代化的全景車廂，從地板到天花板的窗戶提供無障礙的景觀視野。路線穿過伯爾尼高地的心臟地帶，沿途可以看到著名的艾格峰、僧侶峰和少女峰。
5. An 8-Day Panoramic Train Adventure ▾
Interlaken is part of an 8-day journey riding every panoramic train across Switzerland. The team stops in postcard-perfect places along the way, experiencing the country's legendary rail system. From the Golden Pass Express to the funiculars that climb the surrounding peaks, Switzerland's transportation is as much a part of the experience as the destinations. The Swiss don't just drive — they glide, and every journey becomes a scenic adventure.
因特拉肯是為期八天、搭乘每條瑞士全景火車的旅程的一部分。團隊沿途在明信片般完美的地方停留，體驗瑞士傳奇的鐵路系統。從金色山口快車到攀爬周圍山峰的纜車，瑞士的交通和目的地本身一樣是體驗的一部分。瑞士人不只是開車——他們滑行，每一段旅程都變成一場風景冒險。

📐 文法重點 2 點
點選文法點展開說明
It looks [Adj], but + S + [V] + is another story (看起來...，但...是另一回事) ▾
用 'is another story' 表達某事看起來很好但實際做起來又是另一回事，帶有幽默的轉折。
It looks epic, but still leaping off a perfectly solid mountain — I'd probably freeze halfway to the edge.
→ 'looks epic' = 看起來很壯觀 → 用 'but' 轉折引出實際情況：臨陣退縮
It looks + [Adj], but + [實際情況] + is another story
Paragliding looks amazing, ______ (但真的要跳下去是另一回事).
but actually jumping is another story
You're the most + Adj + N + to + V (你是最...的...) ▾
最高級 + 不定詞片語，用來強調某人在某方面是最特別的。
You're the most luckiest couple in the world to be here at this time.
→ 'the luckiest couple in the world' = 世界上最好的情侶 → 'to be here' = 不定詞修飾前面，說明「在哪方面最幸運」
You're the + 最高級 + N + to V
You're the ______ (最勇敢的人) to climb that mountain in this weather.
bravest person
