旅程帶領旅行者來到奧卡萬戈三角洲核心的莫雷米野生動物保護區——一個常被稱為非洲伊甸園的地方。接下來三天，他們的家是卡卡納卡營地，這是知名的 safari 營地之一，坐落在卡卡納卡潟湖邊，四周環繞著豐富的野生動物。往來營地的唯一方式是乘坐小型叢林飛機，從泥土跑道起飛。這是 safari 體驗中奇特且令人難忘的一部分，連飛行本身也成了一場 safari。
2. Introduction: Flying into the Delta ▾
Our journey takes us to the Moremi Game Reserve at the heart of the Okavango Delta — often described as Africa's Garden of Eden. For the next 3 days, our home is Camp Xakanaxa, one of the best-known safari camps, perched on the Xakanaxa Lagoon and surrounded by prime wildlife right in the heart of the reserve. The only way to hop between camps is by small bush planes lifting off dirt airstrips — a quirky, unforgettable part of the safari experience. Flying low, the world stretches wide beneath you. The Okavango Delta unfolds as a horizon-to-horizon masterpiece, painted in water, grass, and light. Even the flight becomes a safari — herds of elephants below, a buffalo herd scattering, hippos breaking the surface. From the air, the Okavango is pure poetry.
在奧卡萬戈三角洲低空飛行時，世界在你腳下寬闊地展開。三角洲像一幅從地平線到地平線的傑作，由水、草和光線繪製而成。它充滿生命力、變幻莫測、無邊無際。成群的大象在下方漫步，水牛群四散奔逃，河馬浮出水面。從空中看，奧卡萬戈就是純粹的詩篇。魔力不僅在於抵達目的地——更在於旅程本身，看著地貌隨著每一分鐘的飛行而變換。
3. Camp Xakanaxa & The Landscape ▾
We're welcomed with warm smiles and voices rising in song. The camp has that out-of-Africa charm — timber buildings blending rustic beauty with modern comfort. The atmosphere is laid-back, unhurried, and timeless. From the lounge and dining rooms, river views stretch wide. It feels authentic, simple yet elegant. The kind of place that slows your pulse the moment you arrive. There are 11 canvas tents here, all raised on decks facing the lagoon. Don't be fooled by "tents" — these have bathrooms, real beds, verandas, and plenty of thoughtful comfort. What really grabs you here isn't just the animals. It's the landscape. The Xakanaxa area hits you with a backdrop that feels almost unreal — a mosaic of water and land. Forests brush up against savannah. Waterways snake through seasonal flood plains. And the best part: you can take it all in with one sweeping look. A single vista that packs every ecosystem into one frame.
抵達時，旅行者們受到溫暖的笑容和歌聲的歡迎。營地散發著真實的「遠離非洲」魅力，木造建築融合了原始之美與現代舒適。氛圍悠閒、從容，彷彿不受時間影響。這裡有11頂帆布帳篷，全都建在面向潟湖的平台上。這些不是普通的帳篷——它們有浴室、真正的床、陽台和許多貼心的舒適設施。感覺真實、簡單卻優雅。
4. Lions & Oscar the Hippo ▾
Safaris deliver something no other journey can: proximity. Being close enough to meet a lion's stare is unsettling, thrilling, unforgettable. He's just there — a predator built for power, yet calm, just lying in the grass beside our vehicle. His brother rolls onto his back like a lazy dog, reminding us this is their normal. For us, it's extraordinary. In this moment, I feel a strange mix of humility and connection. We're not at the zoo. We're in their world. At lunch, we get a visit. Oscar the Hippo — Camp Xakanaxa's most famous resident. That's the closest I've ever been to a hippo. The camp is Oscar's turf. He's a wild hippo evicted from his pod by a dominant male. Instead of going elsewhere, he made it his home. Staff call him the resident mascot, letting him graze freely by the lagoon. He's found safety here. And we found the most unforgettable neighbor a safari camp could have.
卡卡納卡營地的每次遊獵都像一場狩獵。這裡真正吸引人的不僅是動物，還有景觀——水和陸地交織而成的馬賽克。如此近距離地與獅子對視，令人不安、刺激且難以忘懷。午餐時，奧斯卡河馬來訪，他是營地最有名的居民。奧斯卡是一隻被優勢雄性趕出群體的野生河馬，他在營地附近找到了安全之所。
5. Wildlife & The Waterbuck ▾
We've run into plenty of wildlife, but our guide says the floods are higher this year, so sightings aren't as good as usual. Still standing here, I see why they call it the Garden of Eden. Spotting this endangered ground hornbill is special — they breed slowly, just one chick every 9 years. Moremi is among the most stunning safari destinations. Dream-like in beauty, where trees silhouette against the pink skies. Botswana is vast. Endless sky makes every sunrise and sunset unforgettable. A male waterbuck. The white ring on his backside is unmistakable — nature's own bullseye. His coat is oily and foul-smelling, yet he never strays from rivers and marshes. Crocodiles lurk along the water's edge. We spotted something gut-wrenching: a young elephant missing its trunk. Few trunkless calves make it past weaning. Most never see adulthood. Are safaris overrated or unforgettable? Tell us in the comments. We actually read them all.
並非每次目擊都是美好的。旅行者們發現了一頭失去象鼻的小象——很可能是被鱷魚咬掉的。失去象鼻的小象很少能撐過斷奶期，大多數無法長大成年。這是一個令人心痛的事實，提醒人們大自然不是迪士尼電影。然而站在那裡，敘述者明白了為什麼人們稱這裡為伊甸園。奧卡萬戈三角洲提供的是原始、不加修飾的體驗，沒有任何照片能夠真正捕捉。

分詞構句 — 表伴隨狀態與結果 ▾
分詞構句可以簡化句子結構，將兩個動作合併。現在分詞（V-ing）表示主動或同時發生的動作，過去分詞（p.p.）表示被動或已完成。
We're welcomed with warm smiles, voices rising in song before being shown around the camp.
→ 'voices rising in song' = and voices are rising in song（伴隨狀態） → 'before being shown around' = before we were shown around（被動涵義）
S + V + O, N + V-ing... → "伴隨著名詞正在做某事"
Being close enough to meet a lion's stare is unsettling, thrilling, unforgettable.
→ 'Being close enough...' = 動名詞片語當主詞 → 三個形容詞排比加強感受
Being + 形容詞 + enough to + V → "足夠...以至於能夠..."
______ (被美景包圍), we sat in silence for hours.
Surrounded by the beautiful scenery / Being surrounded by the beautiful scenery
倒裝句 — 否定副詞開頭 ▾
當否定詞或具有否定意味的副詞放在句首時，主詞和動詞需要倒裝（aux + S + V）。這在書面英語中非常常見，能增強語氣。
That's the closest I've ever been to a hippo.
→ '...the closest...ever' = 這是我離河馬最近的一次 → 不是倒裝句，但 'ever' 與最高級連用表達經驗
This is the + 最高級 + (that) + S + have ever + p.p. → "這是我所...過最...的"
This is the most beautiful sunset ______ (我見過的).
I have ever seen
