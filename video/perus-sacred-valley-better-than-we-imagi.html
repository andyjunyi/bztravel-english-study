1. The Heart of the Inca World ▾
Peru's Sacred Valley was once the heart of the Inca world. Stretching between Cusco and Machu Picchu, this fertile valley served as an agricultural, spiritual, and political center for the Inca Empire. The travelers flew from Lima to Cusco and took a bus straight into the valley. Because the valley sits at a lower elevation than Cusco, it is an excellent place to acclimatize before heading to higher altitudes. The journey began by exploring ancient terrace farms and paths that once fed an empire.
秘魯的聖谷曾是印加世界的心臟。這片肥沃的山谷位於庫斯科和馬丘比丘之間，是印加帝國的農業、精神和政治中心。旅客從利馬飛到庫斯科，再搭巴士直達山谷。由於山谷海拔比庫斯科低，這裡是適應高海拔環境的絕佳地點。旅程從探索古老的梯田農場和小徑開始，這些曾經養活了一個帝國。
2. Incan Engineering and Terraced Farms ▾
At Moray, the team discovered massive sunken terraces that the Incas engineered for agricultural experiments. Temperatures change drastically across the different stone levels, allowing specific crops to thrive in microclimates. Heading toward the historic site of Pisaq, the valley floor is blanketed in cornfields, and ancient stone terraces line the mountainsides wherever you look. Amazingly, local farmers still use these terraces today, just as their ancestors did centuries ago.
在莫雷，團隊發現了巨大的下沉式梯田，這是印加人為農業實驗而設計的。不同石層的溫度差異極大，使特定作物能在微氣候中茁壯成長。前往皮薩克歷史遺址的路上，谷地鋪滿了玉米田，古老石梯田隨處可見，沿著山坡排列。令人驚嘆的是，當地農民至今仍在使用這些梯田，就像他們的祖先數百年前所做的那樣。
3. Salt Pools and Ancient Traditions ▾
Descending toward the valley, the next stop was Salinas de Maras — an incredible site featuring thousands of terraced salt pools. Locals still use the exact same methods established long before the Inca Empire to harvest this highly prized pink salt. They channel a naturally salty subterranean spring into shallow basins. During the dry season, the intense Andean sun evaporates the water, leaving behind crystallized salt ready for collection. This mineral-rich salt is famous globally for its unique flavor.
往山谷下行，下一站是馬拉斯鹽田——一個擁有數千個梯田鹽池的令人難以置信的遺址。當地人仍然使用早在印加帝國之前就建立的完全相同的方法來採收這種珍貴的粉紅鹽。他們將天然含鹽的地下泉水引入淺盆中。在旱季，強烈的安第斯陽光蒸發水分，留下結晶鹽以供採集。這種富含礦物質的鹽以其獨特風味聞名全球。
4. Chicha, Guinea Pig, and Local Flavors ▾
Driving through rural Peruvian communities, the travelers spotted red plastic bags hanging on wooden poles — a traditional sign advertising a local chicheria. These rustic taverns serve chicha de jora, an ancient indigenous beer brewed from fermented corn. Inside one particularly unique tavern, guinea pigs ran across the dirt floor while locals roasted them whole as a massive delicacy. The travelers also visited Cusco's bustling market, where potatoes of every imaginable variety were piled high — a reminder that Peru is the birthplace of this global crop.
開車經過秘魯鄉村社區時，旅客看到木桿上掛著紅色塑膠袋——這是當地酒館的傳統標誌。這些質樸的小酒館供應奇恰酒，一種用發酵玉米釀造的古老原住民啤酒。在一間極具特色的酒館裡，天竺鼠在泥土地上到處奔跑，當地人將牠們整隻烤熟，視為珍饈。旅客也參觀了庫斯科熙攘的市場，各式各樣的馬鈴薯堆積如山——提醒著人們祕魯是這種全球作物的發源地。
5. Cusco and the Alpaca Farm ▾
Back in Cusco, the ancient capital of Peru, the team explored the colonial architecture, shopped for souvenirs, and took public transport like true locals. Just north of Cusco, they visited a chocolate factory using local organic ingredients. The highlight was visiting an alpaca and llama farm at 12,000 feet. Getting to walk, feed, and pet the adorable animals was an incredible experience. A two-hour-old baby llama, still shaky on its legs, was already following its mother instinctively — a perfect end to an unforgettable journey through the Sacred Valley.
回到庫斯科——秘魯的古都，團隊探索了殖民時期建築、購買紀念品，並像當地人一樣搭乘公共交通。在庫斯科以北，他們參觀了一家使用當地有機食材的巧克力工廠。最精彩的是造訪一個海拔12,000英尺的羊駝和美洲駝農場。能夠近距離走路、餵食和撫摸這些可愛的動物，是一次難以置信的體驗。一隻剛出生兩小時的小美洲駝雖然腳步還有些搖晃，但已經本能地跟隨著母親——這是穿越聖谷這段難忘旅程的完美句點。

分詞片語作原因/時間副詞 (V-ing / Having + p.p.) ▾
分詞片語可以簡化副詞子句。當主句與從句主詞相同時，可以將從句的動詞改為 V-ing 形式。如果動作發生在主句之前，則用 Having + p.p.。
Because the valley is lower in elevation than Cusco, it's a great spot to acclimatize.
→ Being lower in elevation than Cusco, it's a great spot to acclimatize. → 'Being' 簡化了 'Because it is' 所表達的原因關係
Being + Adj..., S + V → 「因為...，所以...」
Descending toward the valley, our next stop was Salinas de Maras.
→ 'Descending' = As we descended... → 分詞片語表示時間上的伴隨動作
V-ing + ..., S + V → 「在...的過程中，...」
______ (因為比庫斯科低), the Sacred Valley is ideal for acclimatization.
Being lower in elevation than Cusco
讓步與對比 — ...while... / while others... ▾
While 可以用來表達對比，意思是「而、然而」，連接兩個形成對比的子句。
Some look perfectly braided while others are short and curly.
→ 'while' 在此表示對比（而），不是時間上的「當...時」 → 用 two-hour-old 和 instinctively follows 形成對比
Some + V..., while others + V... → 「有些...，而其他...」
Some tourists take taxis, ______ (而其他人則搭公車) like locals.
while others take the bus
S + V + Adj + enough to + V (足夠...去做...) ▾
用 'enough to' 結構表達達到某種程度以完成某動作。形容詞放在 enough 前面，後面接不定詞。
The little llama is struggling slightly, but amazingly can stand, walk, and instinctively follows its mother just so soon after birth.
→ 雖然句中沒有直接使用 'enough'，但概念是 'old enough to stand and walk' → 'instinctively follows' 表示與生俱來的能力
S + be + Adj + enough + to V → 「...足夠...以至於能...」
The valley is low enough ______ (讓旅客適應高山環境).
for travelers to acclimatize
