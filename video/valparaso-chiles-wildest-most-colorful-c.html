1. The Casablanca Valley Wine Region ▾
On their way to Valparaíso, Tresa and Zarko stop at the Casablanca Valley, which sits perfectly midway between Santiago and Valparaíso. This area is covered in vineyards producing famous Chilean white wines. The strong marine influence from the Pacific Ocean brings cool breezes, morning mist, and moderate temperatures that create ideal conditions for highly aromatic white wines and fruity reds.
在前往Valparaíso的路上，Tresa和Zarko在Casablanca山谷停留，這個山谷正好位於Santiago和Valparaíso的中間。這個地區遍布葡萄園，生產著名的智利白葡萄酒。來自太平洋的強烈海洋影響帶來涼爽的微風、清晨的薄霧和溫和的溫度，為生產高香氣的白葡萄酒和果味紅酒創造了理想條件。

3. Valparaíso: Art on Every Corner ▾
Valparaíso is described as an unparalleled art lover's paradise. Located about 120 km northwest of Santiago on the central Chilean coast, it's the country's second largest metropolitan area. The city tumbles down 45 hills toward the Pacific Ocean, creating a dramatic and colorful landscape that has inspired artists and creatives for generations.
Valparaíso被形容為無與倫比的藝術愛好者天堂。位於Santiago西北約120公里的智利中部海岸，是該國第二大都市區。這座城市沿著45座山丘向太平洋傾瀉而下，形成了戲劇性且色彩繽紛的景觀，數百年來一直啟發著藝術家和創意人士。
4. A City of Endless Creativity ▾
In Valparaíso, every wall is a canvas, every staircase a mosaic, and every alleyway hides a story. The city's vibrant street art scene is world-famous, with colorful murals covering buildings throughout the hillside neighborhoods. The combination of the ocean backdrop, the colorful houses clinging to steep hills, and the art-filled streets creates an atmosphere unlike anywhere else in Chile.
在Valparaíso，每面牆都是一塊畫布，每段樓梯都是一幅馬賽克，每條小巷都藏著一個故事。這座城市充滿活力的街頭藝術場景世界聞名，色彩繽紛的壁畫遍布整個山丘社區的建築物。海洋背景、依附在陡峭山坡上的彩色房屋以及充滿藝術氣息的街道的結合，營造出智利其他任何地方都無法比擬的氛圍。

Imaginative 'Imagine' for Description ▾
使用祈使句 'Imagine' 來引導觀眾進行想像，創造生動的畫面。這種技巧在旅遊寫作中很常見，能夠讓讀者身臨其境。
Imagine a city that tumbles down 45 hills toward the Pacific Ocean, where every wall is a canvas, every staircase a mosaic, and every alleyway hides a story.
使用 'Imagine a city' 開頭，然後用三個 'every...is...' 的並列結構來描繪城市的特徵。這種排比修辭法創造了強烈的節奏感和畫面感。
Imagine [a place] that [verb] [direction], where [every noun] [is/verb] [description], [every noun] [description], and [every noun] [description].
用 'Imagine' 句型描述一個你喜歡的旅遊目的地。
'Imagine a village that nestles in a green valley, where every house is covered in flowers, every street leads to a view, and every meal is a celebration.'
Active vs. Passive Voice in Descriptions ▾
主動語態和被動語態的交替使用可以豐富寫作風格。主動語態（subject + verb）更直接有力，被動語態（be + past participle）則可以強調動作的接受者。
This entire area is covered in vineyards producing famous Chilean white wines.
被動語態 'is covered in' 強調區域的狀態，而現在分詞 'producing' 則描述了葡萄園的功能。
[Subject] is covered in [noun] [verb-ing] [product].
We happily tasted some excellent Carmenere, Chardonnay, and crisp Sauvignon Blanc.
主動語態 'We happily tasted' 直接描述了他們的品酒體驗，語氣更個人化、更有參與感。
Subject + adverb + verb + [list of items].
將以下句子從被動改為主動：'The wine was tasted by us at the vineyard.'
'We tasted the wine at the vineyard.'
