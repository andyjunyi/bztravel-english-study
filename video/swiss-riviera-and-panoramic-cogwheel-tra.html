1. Montreux: The Swiss Riviera ▾
Welcome to Montreux, where mountains meet the wild breadth of Lake Geneva. The BZ Travel team spends a lazy day on the Swiss Riviera before catching a train up to Zermatt. They wander its fabled promenade — the same path that stirred artists and dreamers for generations. Here, the lake acts like a vast engine of warmth, storing sunlight while the surrounding peaks hold back the northern cold. This fragile balance gives Montreux 20% more sun, where palms, magnolias, and figs thrive at 400 meters.
歡迎來到蒙特勒，群山與廣闊的日內瓦湖在此交會。BZ Travel團隊在瑞士里維埃拉度過悠閒的一天，然後搭火車前往策馬特。他們漫步在傳說中的湖畔步道——同一條啟發了世世代代藝術家和夢想家的路徑。在這裡，湖泊像一台巨大的溫暖引擎，儲存陽光，而周圍的山峰則阻擋了北方的寒冷。這種脆弱的平衡為蒙特勒帶來了多20%的日照，棕櫚樹、木蘭和無花果在海拔400公尺處茁壯生長。
2. Lake Geneva: Western Europe's Largest Alpine Lake ▾
Before the travelers stretches Lake Geneva — 582 square kilometers of shifting light and mist. It's Western Europe's largest alpine lake, and it's so large it creates its own small tides and weather. The team explores Montreux's 7-kilometer lakeside promenade, one of Europe's most scenic, lined with historic mansions, luxury hotels, and gardens facing the calm expanse of the lake. The microclimate here allows subtropical plants to flourish at an altitude where they normally wouldn't survive.
旅客面前的是日內瓦湖——582平方公里的光影與薄霧變幻。這是西歐最大的高山湖泊，它大到能產生自己的小幅潮汐和天氣。團隊探索了蒙特勒7公里長的湖畔步道——歐洲最美麗的步道之一，沿途是歷史悠久的豪宅、豪華酒店和面向寧靜湖面的花園。這裡的微氣候讓亞熱帶植物得以在通常無法存活的緯度茁壯成長。
3. Freddie Mercury's Beloved Escape ▾
Freddie Mercury still stands here in bronze, fist raised toward the Alps. Montreux loved him back. Queen recorded seven albums here, and Freddie claimed this lakeside town as home, watching sunsets from his apartment above the water. What pulled Freddie here? The same thing pulling everyone else — peace. In Montreux, he wasn't Freddie the icon. He was just another guy grabbing coffee and wandering the promenade. Locals didn't swarm him. They let him breathe. That quiet freedom meant everything.
佛萊迪·墨裘瑞的銅像仍然矗立在這裡，拳頭高舉面向阿爾卑斯山。蒙特勒也同樣愛戴他。皇后樂團在這裡錄製了七張專輯，佛萊迪將這個湖畔小鎮視為家，在水上的公寓欣賞日落。是什麼吸引了佛萊迪來到這裡？和吸引所有人的原因一樣——平靜。在蒙特勒，他不是佛萊迪巨星。他只是一個喝咖啡、在步道上漫步的普通人。當地人不會蜂擁而上。他們讓他呼吸。那份寧靜的自由彌足珍貴。
4. The Panoramic Cogwheel Train to Zermatt ▾
After Montreux, the team boards a panoramic cogwheel train heading to Zermatt. The train climbs through breathtaking alpine scenery, passing vineyards, forests, and mountain villages. Cogwheel technology allows the train to tackle steep grades that normal trains cannot handle. As the train ascends, the landscape transforms from Mediterranean lakeside vegetation to alpine forest and eventually to the rocky, snow-capped peaks that define Switzerland's iconic mountain scenery.
離開蒙特勒後，團隊登上前往策馬特的全景齒軌火車。火車穿過令人屏息的阿爾卑斯風景，經過葡萄園、森林和山村。齒軌技術使火車能夠應對普通火車無法處理的陡峭坡度。隨著火車攀升，景觀從地中海式的湖畔植被轉變為高山森林，最終到達構成瑞士標誌性山景的岩石和積雪山峰。
5. An 8-Day Panoramic Train Journey ▾
This journey is part of an 8-day adventure crossing Switzerland by panoramic trains, stopping in breathtaking towns and alpine valleys along the way. From the palm-fringed shores of Lake Geneva to the car-free streets of Zermatt, each segment offers a different chapter of Switzerland's diverse landscape. The contrast between the mild, almost Mediterranean climate of Montreux and the crisp alpine air of Zermatt showcases Switzerland's incredible geographical diversity within a single journey.
這段旅程是為期八天、以全景火車穿越瑞士的冒險之旅的一部分，沿途在令人屏息的小鎮和阿爾卑斯山谷停留。從棕櫚樹環繞的日內瓦湖岸到無車的策馬特街道，每一段都展現了瑞士多樣景觀的不同篇章。蒙特勒溫和近乎地中海的氣候與策馬特清新的高山空氣之間的對比，展示了瑞士在一次旅程中驚人的地理多樣性。

關係副詞 where + 地方描述句 ▾
關係副詞 'where' 用來引導描述地點的形容詞子句，讓句子更優雅，避免重複使用介系詞 + which。
Welcome to Montreux, where mountains meet the wild breadth of Lake Geneva.
→ where = in which 或 at which → 用 where 引出對地點的詩意描述
Welcome to [地點], where + [子句]
Freddie claimed this lakeside town is home, watching sunsets from his apartment above the water.
→ 'watching...' 分詞片語補充說明 → 'where' 也可以改寫為 'from where'
[主句], where + [子句] 描述地點的特色
This is the cafe ______ (在那裡) Freddie Mercury used to drink coffee.
where
It's so + Adj + that + S + V (如此...以至於...) ▾
So...that 結構表達因果關係，強調程度之高導致了某個結果。
Lake Geneva is so large that it creates its own small tides and weather.
→ so large that = 大到...以至於 → 強調湖泊的巨大規模
S + be + so + Adj + that + 結果子句
The air is ______ (如此清新以至於) it feels like you're breathing for the first time.
so fresh that
