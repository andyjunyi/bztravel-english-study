Next, the bohemian heart of Lima, the Barranco district. It's located right next to Miraflores. 7. Pisco Tasting & Plaza de Armas ▾
When you're in Peru, trying Pisco is an absolute must. It's a distilled grape spirit very similar to Italian grappa or our own Bulgarian rakia. But instead of drinking it straight, Peruvians masterly blend it into cocktails. The most famous, of course, is the Pisco Sour. We decided to do a proper Pisco tasting so we could sample a few different variations. So here we are right in the middle of the action, Plaza de Armas — the main square in Lima, the historic core of the City of Kings, dating all the way back to 1535. The presidential palace sits on one side with a huge fence around it, mainly because the square gets pretty lively with protests and political rallies. Over on the right is the Lima Cathedral — absolutely massive, packed with history, and actually where Francisco Pizarro was buried. The coolest part is looking at these bright yellow colonial buildings with amazing dark wooden balconies — a really cool Spanish Moorish vibe. Right in the middle of the modern city: Huaca Pucllana. This is a massive towering pre-Inca archaeological complex made entirely of adobe mud bricks.

不定詞片語表達目的 — To truly understand..., S + V... ▾
不定詞片語放在句首可以表達目的，等於 In order to...。這是一種正式的寫作結構，用來強調行動的動機。
To truly understand the heartbeat of the culture, we hired a private guide.
→ 'To truly understand' = In order to truly understand → 句首不定詞強調目的，主句說明採取的行動
To + V..., S + V... → 「為了...，主詞做...」

這個句型用來表達「如果某人這樣想是可以理解的」，先承認對方的合理假設，再提出反轉的事實。

→ 先承認建築看起來像巴黎搬來的→暗示其實是在利馬 → You'd be forgiven for = 你這樣想是可以被原諒的
